How to Analyze a Chart

1. Start on a high timeframe (1D/4H) to find the macro trend.
2. Identify key Support/Resistance zones.
3. Zoom into lower timeframes (1H/15m) for entry triggers.

🕯️

Top Candlestick Patterns

Engulfing: Strong reversal signal where a candle fully covers the previous body.
Doji: Market indecision. Wait for the next candle to confirm direction.

📉

Understanding RSI & MACD

RSI: >70 is overbought, <30 is oversold.
MACD: Crosses indicate momentum shifts. Divergences warn of reversals.

🛡️

Risk Management 101

Never risk more than 1-2% of your total capital on a single trade. Always aim for a Risk/Reward ratio of at least 1:2. Set strict Stop Losses and stick to them.
